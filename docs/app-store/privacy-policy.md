# Model Pulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Model Pulse ("the app") is designed to show you your own AI provider
usage and spend data while collecting as little information as possible.

## Data the app accesses
- API keys, admin keys, and OAuth tokens you choose to enter, used solely
  to fetch usage, spend, and rate-limit data from the providers you
  connect.
- Usage, spend, and rate-limit data returned by those providers.

## Where data is stored
- Keys and tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain (Secure Enclave-backed)
  on your device.
- Preferences and cached usage data are stored locally on your device.
- Model Pulse does not operate any server. Your data is never sent to us.

## Network requests
The app communicates only with the AI providers you explicitly connect
(for example OpenAI, Anthropic, Moonshot) using their official APIs, and
only with the credentials you provide.

## Analytics, tracking, and ads
None. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking SDKs,
and no third-party data sharing.

## Data deletion
Delete the app to remove all locally stored data. Keys can also be
removed individually in the app's settings.
